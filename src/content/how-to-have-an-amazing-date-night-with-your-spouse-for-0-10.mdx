### **1\. Set the Scene**

**Plan Ahead:** Decide on a day and time that works for both of you. Make this an exclusive date, just like you would if you were heading to a high-end restaurant or show.

**Dress the Part:** While the point is to find hilarious outfits at the thrift shop, make a fun game out of your starting outfit. Maybe opt for the “what I think my spouse will pick for me” attire. This way, the fun begins even before you reach the store!

### **2\. Thrift Shop Adventures**

**The Ugly Outfit Challenge:** This is where the fun begins. Split up and give each other a set amount of time (say 10-15 minutes) to pick the most outrageous outfit for the other person. The tackier, the better!

**Photo-Op:** Once you’ve both picked an outfit, try them on. Capture the moment with a series of silly photos. Pose dramatically, catwalk, or do a fake magazine cover shoot. These photos will be a great keepsake to look back on and laugh together.

**Rate the Outfits:** Take turns describing what you love about the chosen ensemble. Maybe it’s the 80’s neon colors, the overly large shoulder pads, or those sequined pants. Remember, it’s all in good fun!

### **3\. Share the Laughs**

**Picnic in the Dressing Room:** While trying on clothes, take a short break for a snack. Pack a small bag of homemade popcorn or a couple of sandwiches. It adds an extra special touch to your thrift shop adventure.

**Fashion Show:** If you’re up for it and the store allows, why not have a mini fashion show? Strut down the aisles, strike a pose, and let your spouse be the judge.

### **4\. Make a Decision**

**To Buy or Not to Buy:** You’ve had your fun and laughs, but now comes the decision. If you’ve truly fallen in love with a particular piece (or if you want a hilarious memento from the date), consider buying it. Often, these thrift store finds will be less than $10.

**Donation:** Another beautiful twist to this date idea could be to bring some of your old clothes and donate them. It’s a way of giving back and making room for any new treasures you might find.

### **5\. Wind Down and Reflect**

**Recap at Home:** Once you’re home, go through the photos together. Maybe even post a couple of your favorites on social media to share the joy.

**Plan for the Next:** The best part about this date idea is that there are countless thrift shops and endless ugly outfits to try. So, always be on the lookout for the next opportunity.

**Go have fun with your spouse – CHEAP!  
**Having an unforgettable date night doesn’t mean you have to spend a fortune. It’s about the memories you create, the laughter you share, and the bond you strengthen with your spouse. So, head to your local thrift shop and let the fashion adventure begin!
