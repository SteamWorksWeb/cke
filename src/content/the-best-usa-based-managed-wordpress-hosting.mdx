## Kinsta

![](/images/blog/kinsta-1024x636.jpg)

Kinsta is a premium WordPress hosting provider that offers a fully managed platform. Their hosting plans include daily backups, 24/7 support, unlimited traffic, and free SSL certificates. They also have extremely powerful development tools available for people and companies that need them.

Kinsta is all USA based and supported with their customer service team located in Los Angeles and other parts of the United States.

They are one of the few hosts that owns and operates their own data centers located across the globe. This gives them complete control over their infrastructure and allows them to offer a 99.9% uptime guarantee.

Kinsta’s features are second to none, making it the best fully managed WordPress host on the market today.

Clay has tried just about every website host on the planet over the past 18 years and for the past 3 years, Clay has called Kinsta home for himself and also his clients. The power, the tools, the page load speeds, and the amazing customer support are worth every penny. 

[CHECK OUT KINSTA](http://kinsta.com)

## Flywheel

![](/images/blog/flywheel-1024x557.jpg)

In the world of WordPress hosting, there are a few big players that everyone knows about. However, there are also a number of quality budget-friendly options that often get overlooked. Flywheel is one of these hidden gems.

For starters, Flywheel offers a generous discount for users who sign up for an annual plan. They also offer a free site migration service, which can be a lifesaver if you’re switching hosts. In addition, Flywheel provides an easy-to-use control panel and excellent al U.S. customer support.

Their uptime is also rock-solid, so you can rest assured that your site will always be up and running. If you’re looking for a quality budget-friendly WordPress host, Flywheel is definitely worth checking out.  
  
Clay used Flywheel for about 5 years and for most websites, they are great. But, for Clay, some of his client sites had outgrown the server power they offer so he moved all his sites to Kinsta.  
  
Still, Flywheel is AWESOME and their support is second to none. If you have a small to medium website, this is your best budget-friendly choice. 

[CHECK OUT FLYWHEEL](http://getflywheel.com)

## WP Engine

![](/images/blog/wp-engine-1024x491.jpg)

WP Engine is a popular managed WordPress host that offers great tools, server power, and customer support. One of the main reasons people choose WP Engine is because it offers an all-in-one solution for WordPress hosting. This means that you don’t have to worry about configuring your own server or installing and maintaining WordPress yourself. WP Engine takes care of all of that for you.

In addition, WP Engine offers a number of great features such as automatic backups, one-click restore points, and staging areas. These features make it easy to manage your WordPress site and keep it running smoothly. If you’re looking for a managed WordPress host that can take care of everything for you, WP Engine is a great option to consider.

WP Engine recently bought Flywheel. So, technically, they are the same company. However, they operate independently (as of this writing). 

[CHECK OUT WP ENGINE](http://wpengine.com)
