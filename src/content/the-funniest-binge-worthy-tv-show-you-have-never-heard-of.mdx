## What we do in the Shadows

This show is worth the watch — especially if you like comedies based on what should be serious subjects. lol. That is, if you can call vampires a serious subject. 

There are 4 seasons of the show that you can binge watch on FX. I personally use YouTube TV so all the episodes are available. You might be able to find it on Hulu or some other platform. Not sure, you’ll have to check. 

But the show is hilarious. It is filmed as if it is a documentary on vampires — which makes it even funnier.  Lots of witty comments and lines, lots of crazy storylines, and great characters. It’s worth the watch if you are in need of a laugh.
