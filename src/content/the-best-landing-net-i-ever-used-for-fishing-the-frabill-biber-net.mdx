Compact and Durable

Fishing is an exciting and challenging sport, and having the right equipment can make all the difference. One tool that is often overlooked but is crucial to a successful fishing trip is a landing net. A high-quality net can mean the difference between landing a trophy redfish or bass, or losing it. That’s why I want to introduce you to the Frabill Hiber-Net, a fishing net that offers a unique and innovative approach to traditional collapsible fishing nets.

![](/images/blog/frabill-hiber-net2.jpg)

As a charter captain for 10+ years, having a landing net was essential but landing nets are cumbersome, hard to store, and always in the way. One year, i found the solution! The Frabill Hiber-Net! I ended up using it for several years and it was easy to store and amazingly durable. I just recently bought one for my son for bass fishing and he’s going to love it. 

Frabill is a company with a rich history of 80 years in the fishing industry. Their commitment to innovation and creativity is evident in their range of fishing accessories, including nets, rainwear, and live bait gear. The Hiber-Net is a prime example of their “think outside the box” design philosophy.

What sets the Hiber-Net apart from other collapsible fishing nets is its unique mechanism. Unlike other nets that fold with hinges, the Hiber-Net’s net hoop retracts into the handle, resulting in a more compact storage solution. This design eliminates the challenges associated with stowing large nets, particularly on smaller boats where space is limited. The folded Hiber-Net becomes a small pole that can easily be stored in side pockets or the cabin, while still offering a perfect lenth for landing fish when the hoop is extended.

When it comes to performance, the Hiber-Net exceeded my expectations during my years as a charter captain in the Tamp Bay area in saltwater conditions. I put it to the test landing redfish, snook, speckled sea trout, cobia and more. The sliding hard plastic handle allowed for quick, simple operation, and the locking mechanism required minimal force. The net extends and retracts smoothly, making it easy to use even in high-pressure situations.

In terms of durability, the Hiber-Net is built to last. After several years and hundreds of fishing trips in saltwater conditions, there were no signs of corrosion. The net is constructed with high-quality materials and offers anglers a reliable and convenient solution for landing their catch. I have no doubt it will serve my son well for many years in tthe freshwater environment he fishes.

For anglers who would enjoy having a net that collapses into an easy to store package, the Hiber-Net is a great choice. Its innovative design and solid construction make it a must-have for those seeking a high-quality landing net. The compact size and ease of use make it a great option for anglers who fish on small boats with limited storage. It’s perfect for bass anglers and inshore anglers.

Whether you’re looking for a compact storage solution, a net that can handle large fish, or a fishing net that is easy to use, the Frabill Hiber-Net is the answer. So, if you’re looking for a fishing net that will help you land your next trophy fish, be sure to check out the Frabill Hiber-Net.
