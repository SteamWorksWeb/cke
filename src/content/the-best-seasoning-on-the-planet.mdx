## Slap Ya Mamma

![](/images/blog/Slap-Ya-Mamma.jpg)

I’ve always been kind of a foodie. And, I especially love seasonings and hot sauces. Hands down, the most versatile seasoning on the planet with the best flavor is Slap Ya Mamma. It works on EVERYTHING. Fish, chicken, shrimp, steak, buttered Italian toast, salad, wings, BBQ, eggs, hashbrowns…. the list goes on and on. 

It has just enough salt for flavor and just enough kick for those that like just a little spice. Be careful using it though because a little goes a long way. One can of Slap Ya Mamma will last you quite a while. But dang…. nothing tastes better! Add it to your favorite dish and see what happens. I have told so many friends about this — and once they got some, the response is always the same. They love it!

They do make some other flavors like a “Hot” version and a “Pepper” version, but the original is by far my favorite.  

Go on! **Slap Ya Mamma!**
