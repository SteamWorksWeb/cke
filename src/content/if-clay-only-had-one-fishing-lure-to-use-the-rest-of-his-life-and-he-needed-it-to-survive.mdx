## A "Fluke" or soft plastic "Jerkbait"

![](/images/blog/do-they-catch-1024x989.jpg)

There is only one fishing lure I would choose if I was stranded on a desert island or in the wilderness with lakes and that is the fluke-style bait (also called a “jerkbait”)

The bait can be fished in a multitude of different ways — including weightless, weedless, on a jighead, dropshot, and many other ways… 

I personally have caught these fish on a fluke-style soft plastic bait. 

*   Redfish (red drum)
*   Speckled sea trout
*   Sheepshead
*   Largemouth Bass
*   Tarpon
*   Spanish Mackerel
*   Bluefish
*   Snook
*   Spotted bass
*   Catfish
*   Black drum
*   Mangrove snapper
*   Ladyfish

It is a versatile lure that mimics the action of a baitfish. So, many types of fish are attracted to it. If I had to pick one lure to try to survive and feed myself with, it would be a fluke/jerkbait.
