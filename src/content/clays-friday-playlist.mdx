## Here's what I'm listening to today.

It’s Friday, and I’m ready to relax this weekend. Here are the bands on my playlist that I’m listening to as I power through the workday. With a mix of country, rock, and everything in between, these 10 bands are the perfect soundtrack for getting me through the day and into the weekend ahead. From Koe Wetzel’s high-energy country anthems to Whiskey Myers’ classic southern rock sound, each band on this list has something unique to offer, and I can’t wait to dive in and let the music carry me away. So let’s turn up the volume, forget about our troubles, and get ready to have some fun, because it’s Friday, and anything is possible.

## Koe Wetzel

Koe Wetzel and his band are known for their high-energy, rock-infused country music. Their sound is perfect for blasting on a Friday night, and their lyrics often touch on themes of love, heartbreak, and partying. Some of their most popular songs include “February 28, 2016,” “Ragweed,” and “Austin.”

## Jelly Roll

This Tennessee-based rapper blends country, rock, and hip-hop to create a sound that is both unique and infectious. His lyrics often touch on themes of struggle, redemption, and overcoming obstacles. Some of his most popular songs include “Save Me,” “I Need You,” and “Demons.”

## Treaty Oak Revival

This Austin-based band is steeped in the traditions of classic country and western swing, with a sound that is both timeless and modern. Their music often features lively fiddle and guitar solos, along with catchy choruses that will have you tapping your toes. Some of their most popular songs include “Night Time in Old San Antone,” “Cowboys and Indians,” and “I Don’t Care.”

## HARDY

This Mississippi-born singer-songwriter has become one of the hottest new stars in country music, with a sound that is equal parts rock and country. His music often features heavy guitar riffs and catchy choruses that are impossible not to sing along to. Some of his most popular songs include “ONE BEER,” “Give Heaven Some Hell,” and “Boyfriend.”

## Them Dirty Roses

This Alabama-based band has a classic southern rock sound, with a heavy emphasis on guitar riffs and catchy hooks. Their music is perfect for letting loose and having a good time, with lyrics that often touch on themes of partying, love, and life on the road. Some of their most popular songs include “Where The Devil Stays,” “Rattlesnake,” and “Down That Road.”

## Cross Canadian Ragweed

This Oklahoma-based band is widely regarded as one of the pioneers of the red dirt music movement. Their sound blends elements of rock, country, and blues to create a sound that is uniquely their own. Their music often features heavy guitar riffs, catchy choruses, and lyrics that touch on themes of love, heartbreak, and life on the road. Some of their most popular songs include “Alabama,” “Cry Lonely,” and “17.”

## Dylan Wheeler

This up-and-coming singer-songwriter from Indiana has a sound that is equal parts country and rock. His music often features heavy guitar riffs and catchy choruses, with lyrics that touch on themes of love, heartbreak, and living life to the fullest. Some of his most popular songs include “Tell Me If I’m Wrong,” “Tell Me If I’m Right,” and “When She Says Baby.”

## Atlas

This Los Angeles-based band has a sound that is hard to define, blending elements of rock, pop, and electronic music to create something truly unique. Their music often features catchy hooks, lush production, and lyrics that touch on themes of love, loss, and the human experience. Some of their most popular songs include “Bittersweet,” “Broken Record,” and “Save Me.”

## Whiskey Myers

This Texas-based band has a sound that is steeped in the traditions of classic southern rock, with a modern twist. Their music often features heavy guitar riffs, catchy choruses, and lyrics that touch on themes of love, heartbreak, and life on the road. Some of their most popular songs include “Ballad of a Southern Man,” “Stone,” and “Die Rockin’.”
